Tailoring and Cutting (Fashion Designing)

Unlock the world of fashion with our Tailoring and Cutting course. Master the art of creating bespoke garments, from pattern making to final stitching, for a career in haute couture or bespoke fashion.

Course Modules

Pattern Making
  • Introduction to Pattern Making
  • Measuring and Fitting Techniques
  • Creating Custom Patterns
Tailoring and Garment Construction
  • Fabric Selection and Preparation
  • Tailoring Techniques for Various Fabrics
  • Garment Construction and Finishing
Fashion Design and Styling
  • Introduction to Fashion Design Principles
  • Styling and Trend Forecasting
  • Creating a Fashion Portfolio
Fashion Photography and Portfolio Development
  • Introduction to Fashion Photography
  • Building a Professional Portfolio
  • Editing and Post-Production Techniques

Course Syllabus

Section A: Basic Tailoring
Unit Contents Theory Practical
1 Introduction to Sewing Machine Machine Operation, Parts, Safety, Maintenance Operating, Removing/Refixing Parts, Cleaning/Oiling
2 Tools and Equipments Description of Drafting, Cutting, Stitching Tools Practice with Scissors, Shapper, L Scale, etc.
3 Trade Terminology Terms for Drafting, Cutting, Stitching Practice Using Proper Terms
4 Measurement Taking Tape Measurement, L Scale, Precautions Practice with Tape, L Scale, Sequencing
5 Drafting/Pattern Making Need, Method, Simple Drafting, Pattern Making Paper Pattern Cutting
6 Basic Stitching Hand Stitches, Machine Stitches, Decorative Stitches Sample Pieces of Stitches
7 Construction Skills Seams, Drafts, Tucks, Pleats, etc. Sample Pieces Using Construction Skills
8 Selection of Cloth Selecting Right Fabric, Identification
9 Estimation of Cloth Estimation by Measurements Practical Estimation Exercises
10 Colour Concept Colour Combination, Contrast, Harmony Practical Colour Selection
11 Simple Cutting and Stitching Marking, Leaving Margin, Stitching Skills Marking, Cutting, Stitching Practices
